Getting Started

The Travel Application is part of Smart Office. You need to have access to Smart Office to be able to use the Travel Application and the Travel Reimbursement form. If you do not have access to Smart Office, contact the I.T. Department to set up the account for you.

Contact IT Department

Travel Application General Information

How to navigate to the Travel Application

Log on to the Smart Office website with your login credentials here: foxoffice.fii-usa.com (if this link does not work, go to this url: http://10.20.193.66/ You will see the web page shown below:

Once you are successfully logged in, you will be directed to the Smart Office Dashboard with different modules to choose from. To navigate to the Travel Application, first click on the START HERE button or scroll down.

Click on the Travel Forms Module

This is the Travel Application home page. Click “CREATE TRAVEL APPLICATION”

And now you have successfully navigated to the Travel Application!

How to Create a Travel Application

Now that you know how to get to the travel application, let’s figure out how to save and submit it, and what all of the fields are for. Use the Next Button or the timeline above the form to navigate through the different pages of the form.

The next few pages will be describing the different fields on the form.

Personal Information (Image Shown Above)
  • Company: The Company that you belong to
  • Form ID: No need to worry about this one, it just helps us keep track of the forms. If you are just creating a Travel Application, it will be blank. But if you are looking at a saved or submitted Travel Application, it will show the form ID.
  • Business Group: The Business Group that you belong to
  • Employee Name: Auto-filled to be your name
  • BU (Profit Center): Auto-filled to be your Business Unit
  • Department: Auto-filled to be your department
  • Project: The project that you are traveling for
  • Travel Type: Are you going to be traveling internationally or domestically?
  • Estimated Expense: Automatically calculates the estimated expense of the trip based on the numbers you put into the form.
  • Cost Center Description: Defaults to your Cost Center. If it needs to be changed for whatever reason, you can use it to change the cost center in charge of your trip.
  • Budget Source Code: Automatically fills to whatever the budget source code is for the Cost Center Selected in the Cost Center Description Select Box
Travel Information (Image Shown below)
  1. Travel Date: The day you will start to travel. It cannot be today or any day before today.
  2. Travel To: The day you will come back from your travels.
  3. Estimated Period: Automatically calculates how many days you will be away
  4. Transportation: Would you be getting there by flight, train, or rental car?
  5. Departure (Country): What country you will be traveling from?
  6. Departure Factory Code: The factory you are associated with
  7. Departure State/City(Preferred): Where would you prefer to depart for your travels?
  8. Destination(Country): The country you will be traveling to.
  9. Destination (State): The state or province you will be traveling to.
  10. Destination City: The city you will be traveling to.
  11. Accommodation: Will you be in a hotel or a dormitory?
  12. Currency: The form of currency that you will need for the trip

Travel Purpose (Image Shown Below)
  • Date: The date of the event
  • Visit Company: The Company you will visit
  • Department: The Department you work with
  • Contact: The person you will be working with
  • Precise work Item or Objective: What are you planning to achieve?
  • Add Row: Click this to add a new row to the table if you need to add more information
  • Delete Row: To delete a row in the table, check the checkbox next to the row and then click the Delete Row button.

Expense (Image Shown Below)
  • Airway Ticket: How much your plane ticket will cost in US dollars. If you will not be taking a plane, leave it at 0.
  • Explanation (Airway Ticket): Explanation for why ticket costs what it does (where you are getting it from, how many tickets, ect.)
  • Accommodation: How much it will cost to stay at where you are staying.
  • Explanation (Accommodation/Hotel Options): This button shows you some options of where you can stay.
  • Transportation: The cost of transportation (besides your plane ticket)
  • Explanation (Transportation): The Enterprise Rental Code for your transportation
  • Per Diem/Allowance Per Diem: You can leave it blank, or choose whichever country that you will be visiting and the per diem amount that you receive will be calculated for you.
  • Explanation (Per Diem): Auto-fills based on per diem value chosen
  • Public Relationship Expense: How much you think you will spend on a customer
  • Explanation (Public Relationship Expense): Why you will spend this much on a customer
  • Other Expense: Any other expenses you will have
  • Explanation (other expense): What are the other expenses
  • Total Expenses: Automatically calculated based on the numbers entered on the other expense fields

Travel Advance (Image Shown Below)

To apply for travel advance, click on Apply for Travel Advance Application on the bottom of page 3. Note: If you do not have a vendor code yet, you will need to get one if you want to apply for a travel advance amount

There is no vendor code attached to this account: means that you need to get a vendor code if you want to apply for travel advance. If you do have a vendor code but it says that you do not, please contact IT.

Advance Amount: The only field you need to worry about if you are not an accountant. The advance amount cannot be greater than the total expenses of the trip.

Travel Policy (Image Shown Below)

Read the policy, then check the checkbox that says ‘I have read and Understand the Foxconn Travel Policy’

The green Send button will be enabled. When you click it, the form will be submitted.

Saving the Travel Application

If you cannot finish the travel application, or you do not know all of the details about all of the required fields, you can click the Save and Return to Home button on the bottom of the application. This will take you back to the dashboard

To find your saved Travel Application from the dashboard, click again on the Travel Forms module

Then, click on “View my Travel App”

Next, click on the Travel Application Menu and choose ‘View my Applications’

This will take you to a page with all your travel applications on the website. Forms that are not submitted will be at app stage 0 and will say that they need to be submitted

To continue editing a saved form, click on it in the list. It will bring you to the saved form and you can continue editing it

Monitoring Your Travel Application

This page has general information about all your travel applications. You can see if the travel application is approved, pending, or declined, and what stage of approval the form is currently on (as Shown in the Image above). If you click on a form that is submitted, it will show you the form, but all the fields will be greyed-out. This is because once a form is submitted for approval, it cannot be edited by the creator anymore.

If you go to the timeline on the top, and click on ‘Approve’, it will take you to a page that shows the actions each approver has done to the form.

Travel Application Approval Process

There are multiple ways that you can find travel applications that you need to approve.

Check your email
When it is your turn to approve a form, an email will be sent to you with the link to the form that you need to approve. Click on the link and it will take you to the form that you need to approve

Check your notifications on the dashboard
On the dashboard, there is a little white bell on the top left corner. If there is a red number next to it, you can click on it and see your notifications.
When you need to approve a form, a notification will be given to you. If you click on the specific notification, it will take you to the form that you need to approve.
Note: when if you click on a notification, it will disappear from your notifications.

Go to the ‘Approve Forms’ page for Travel Applications
To get to the approve forms page from the Dashboard, go to the travel forms page.
Then click on Travel Application Menu to open the dropdown and click ‘Approve Applications’

This will take you to a page that has all the Travel Applications that you have approved and that you need to approve.
The ones you need to approve will have ‘Action Needed’ under Action Required. When you click on the form, it will take you to the page where you can approve the form.

Approving / Declining a Travel Application

When you get to the application you need to approve, go to the last page of the form.

You can type a comment in the comments box. When you are ready to approve the form, you can click the Approve button. You will be redirected to the Dashboard and an email will be sent to the next approver.

OR to Decline

You can type a comment in the comments box. When you are ready to decline the form, you can click the Decline button. You will be redirected to the Dashboard and the creator of the form will be notified of their form being declined.

Requesting Modifications to a Travel Application

When you get to the application you need to modify, go to the last page of the form.

You can type a comment in the comments box. When you are ready to modify the form, you can click the Modify button. You will then be able to go back to the rest of the form and change the information on the form. When you are finished with your modifications, click the button again.

The request will be sent to the original creator of the form, and they will choose to either accept or decline your modifications. If they decline your modifications, you will be notified and the form will go back to you. If they accept the modifications, the form will go to the person after you in the approval process.

Editing the Approver List

When you get to the application you need to modify, go to the last page of the form. Click the green ‘Edit Approver List’ button.

You can then choose between three actions:

  • Replace yourself with someone else in the approval process
  • Remove yourself from the approval process
  • Add someone to the approval process

The request will be sent to the original creator of the form, and they will choose to either accept or decline your modifications. If they decline your modifications, you will be notified and the form will go back to you. If they accept the modifications, the form will go to the person after you in the approval process.

If your travel application gets declined

When your form is declined you will get an email and a notification with a link to the form telling you that your form was declined. To view the declined form, click on the link in the email or the notification on the dashboard.

When you get to the form you may notice two buttons.
One says ‘Restart with a new form’, and the other says ‘Use as base for new form’. If you click on ‘Restart with a new form, all the data you entered from the declined form will be cleared and you can re-write everything. If you click ‘Use as base for new form’, the fields will remain unchanged except you will be able to edit them. After this, you can save or submit the form.

When Travel Application Form is Completely Approved

When the travel form is completely approved, you will get an email and a notification on the website that it was completely approved.
You can click on the notification or the link in the email to view your approved travel application. You may notice that you can now apply for reimbursement.

How to Create a Travel Reimbursement from

In order for you to create a new travel reimbursement form, you must have at least one completely approved Travel Application that does not yet have a travel reimbursement form attached to it.

To create the travel reimbursement form, go to your completely approved travel application. On the application there will be a button that says: ‘Apply for Reimbursement’. Click on it to create the travel reimbursement form.

You will be redirected to a page that will allow you to fill out your travel reimbursement form. Use the Next Button or the timeline above the form to navigate through the different pages of the form.

As the Applicant, you won’t have to worry about these fields too much because they are all greyed-out and auto-filled by the computer based on your employee information and the travel application associated with the travel reimbursement form.

Mileage Buttons

Edit Button

Click on this button to view the popup that contains all the fields you need to fill out about the week

Delete Week Button

Deletes all weeks in the section that are checked off, but it cannot delete every week. There must be at least one.

Add New Week:

Adds a new week to the form for the specified section.

Mileage Expenses

To fill out your mileage expenses, you must click the Edit button on the week you want to fill out information on. The popup below will appear. Fill in the information for each day of the week. If you did not travel on that day, leave it blank. If you fill out mileage or amount for a day, you will have to also tell where you traveled to and from.

Personal Meal

To fill out the personal meal expenses for the week, you must click the Edit button on the week you want to fill out information on. The popup below will appear. Fill in the information for each day of the week. If you were not away on travel during that day, or you did not eat during that meal, or the meal you had was not considered a personal meal, but instead a business meal, leave it blank.

You can choose between filling out how much you spent on personal meals for each meal... OR if you chose the per diem option on the travel application you can fill-in that value for each day in the per diem section.

Per Diem: Can be filled out INSTEAD of filling out how much you spent for each meal. You could fill these out instead of the individual meals if you chose a per diem expense on your travel application.

Sub Total $: Automatically calculates how much money you spent on personal meals for the whole week

Hotel Expenses

To fill out the personal meal expenses for the week, you must click the Edit button on the week you want to fill out information on. The popup below will appear. Fill in the information for each day of the week. If you were not away on travel during that day or did not spend any money on the specific expense, leave it blank. .

Expenditure to be charged to Company

Week Start Date: The start date of the week that you will be filling out other expenditure information for. Requirements: It must be a Monday. If you did not start traveling on a Monday, choose the Monday before you started traveling.

Upload Document

You need to prove your expenses on your trip(Mileage Document, Personal Expense, Business Expenses) by uploading a supporting document. Click the Choose File button to choose a file from your computer and upload it as proof of your mileage.

Saving your Travel Reimbursement Form

If you want to save your Travel Reimbursement form, but it is not ready to submit yet, you can press the ‘Save’ button on the bottom of the form. Note: In order to save the form, you will need to have all date fields filled-out. You can change them later if they are incorrect

You will then be redirected to a page (as shown below) that has a list of all your travel reimbursement forms. Go to this page whenever you want to check on your travel reimbursement forms or work on a travel reimbursement that has not been saved yet.

Monitoring Your Travel Reimbursement Form

Not all the travel reimbursement forms on the View Your Travel Reimbursement Forms Page are unsubmitted, and some of them are submitted. Each form has general information about it on this page. You can see if the form is approved, pending, or declined, and what stage of approval the form is currently on.

If you go to the timeline on the top, and click on ‘Approve’, it will take you to a page that shows the actions each approver has done to the form
